The Client App — Your Secret Weapon

Your clients do the admin for you.

SAY-OS includes a free client app that connects directly to your salon system. Clients fill in their profile once — you benefit every visit. Every Beauty Passport entry gives you better data. Every Health Profile reduces your liability. Every Skincare Routine entry prevents a bad reaction.

✨
Beauty Passport
Clients store their colour formulas, style preferences, allergies, and service history. Your team sees it all before every appointment. No clipboard, no guessing, no "what did we do last time?"
🩺
Health Profile
Skin type, conditions, contraindications, patch tests — all entered by the client, flagged automatically to you. Reduces your liability before the client sits in the chair.
🧴
Skincare Routine Tracker
Clients log their home products and ingredients. You see potential conflicts before you apply anything. Retinol before a peel? Flagged. AHA overload? Flagged.
🗓️
Event Prep Planner
Wedding, holiday, event — automated prep timelines with treatment scheduling. Clients arrive looking their best with zero guesswork on timing.
🌍
Travel Passport
Clients visiting from abroad share their full beauty profile via QR code. Complete history, no intake form, no language barrier — translated into 14 languages, 82 beauty terms. The tourist in your chair is no longer a stranger.
